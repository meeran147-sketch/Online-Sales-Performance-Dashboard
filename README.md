# Online-Sales-Performance-Dashboard (Interactive Dashboard Creation using MS Excel)
## Project Objective
The objective of this Online Sales Performance Dashboard is to analyze and monitor key sales metrics, including total sales revenue, orders, quantity sold, and discounts across different regions and sales representatives. The dashboard provides an interactive view of monthly sales trends, regional performance, and unit price distribution, enabling stakeholders to identify sales patterns, evaluate business performance, and make data-driven decisions. By visualizing critical KPIs and allowing dynamic filtering by region, sales representative, and date, the dashboard helps improve sales strategy, optimize resource allocation, and enhance overall business growth.

## Question
- What is the total sales revenue generated?
- How many orders were placed during the selected period?
- What is the total quantity of products sold?
- What is the total discount provided to customers?
- Which region generates the highest sales revenue?
- How does sales performance compare across different regions?
- What is the unit price contribution by each region?
- What are the monthly sales trends throughout the year?
- Which month recorded the highest and lowest sales?
- How do sales and unit prices relate across regions?
- Which sales representative contributes the most to overall sales?
- How do sales performance metrics change when filtered by region, sales representative, or date?

## Process
- Collected online sales data containing orders, sales, quantity, discounts, regions, and sales representatives.
- Removed duplicate records.
- Handled missing values and corrected data inconsistencies.
- Formatted date and numerical fields.
- Data Preparation
- Created calculated fields for Total Sales, Total Orders, Quantity Sold, and Discounts.
- Organized data into a structured tabular format.
- Created Pivot Tables to summarize sales performance by month, region, and sales representative.
- Calculated key performance indicators (KPIs).
- Developed KPI cards for Total Sales, Orders, Quantity, and Discounts.
- Created charts for monthly trends, regional sales comparison, and unit price analysis.
- Added slicers for Region, Sales Representative, and Order Date.
- Enabled dynamic filtering for user-driven analysis.
- Identified top-performing regions and sales trends.
- Generated actionable insights to support business decisions and revenue growth.
